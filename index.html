Who We Are

San Gabriel Valley Power Up Beacon is a beacon of hope and progress, dedicated to fostering a thriving and equitable San Gabriel Valley for all.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities in the San Gabriel Valley through accessible education, vital resources, and collaborative initiatives that foster sustainable growth and well-being.

Our Vision

A San Gabriel Valley where every resident has the opportunity to thrive, supported by a strong community network and innovative solutions to local challenges.
