About San Gabriel Valley Power Up Beacon

Dedicated to fostering a thriving and equitable San Gabriel Valley.

Our Story

Discover the heart and soul of the San Gabriel Valley Power Up Beacon.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities in the San Gabriel Valley through accessible education, vital resources, and collaborative initiatives that foster sustainable growth and well-being. We strive to build a supportive ecosystem where everyone has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

A San Gabriel Valley where every resident thrives, supported by a strong community network, equitable access to opportunities, and innovative solutions to local challenges. We envision a future where our communities are resilient, connected, and prosperous for generations to come.

Our Values

  • Community: We believe in the power of collective action and strong community bonds.
  • Empowerment: We equip individuals with the tools and knowledge to achieve their goals.
  • Equity: We are committed to creating fair opportunities for all.
  • Collaboration: We partner with local organizations and residents to maximize our impact.
  • Innovation: We embrace creative solutions to address evolving community needs.
